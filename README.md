# Squad AI
 
**Agentic AI, knowledge graphs, and entity extraction for defence, government, and regulated enterprise.**
 
We build pipelines that ingest unstructured documents, extract entities and relationships, and structure them into queryable knowledge graphs — enabling organisations to surface intelligence from complex data at speed.
 
## What we do
 
- **Entity extraction** — Automated identification of people, organisations, locations, and relationships from unstructured text
- **Knowledge graph construction** — Structured, queryable graphs built from raw documents
- **Agentic AI workflows** — Multi-step analytical pipelines that automate complex tasks with full auditability
- **Air-gapped deployment** — Built for environments where data cannot leave the network
 
## Built for sensitive environments
 
- 🇬🇧 British company with UK data residency
- Security-cleared team with appropriate vetting for sensitive environments
- Human-in-the-loop controls — every decision explainable and auditable
- Listed on [G-Cloud 15](https://www.digitalmarketplace.service.gov.uk/)
 
## Our stack
 
We work with Neo4j, Python, and large language models. Our architecture is designed for sovereign, air-gapped environments where data security and auditability are non-negotiable.
